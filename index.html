Privacy Policy for Mission Maths

Last Updated: July 13, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Mission Maths ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application "Mission Maths" (the "App").

1. No Data Collection

Mission Maths is designed to be a completely safe and private experience. We do not collect, store, use, or share any personal information or non-personal data from our users.

Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal names, email addresses, or contact information.
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data.
  • Any usage analytics, gameplay statistics, or other data related to your interaction with the App.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores some data directly on your device to save your progress. This data includes:

  • Your high scores.
  • Your unlocked achievements.
  • Your game settings (e.g., volume preferences).

This information is stored **only on your device** and is never transmitted to us or any third party. If you uninstall the App, this data will be permanently deleted.

3. No Ads or In-App Purchases

The App is completely free of advertisements and does not contain any in-app purchases.

4. Children's Privacy

Our App is designed for a general audience, including children. As we do not collect any personal information, we are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other privacy regulations. We provide a safe, self-contained environment for learning and fun.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
